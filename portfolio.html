Project 1.2.5.

This is my game called "Reaction time." The game first asks you to input your name, after you enter that it asks you how many attempts you want. After that you press the start game and you start clicking the fish as they pop up on the screan. The score is calculatted by the distance from fish to fish on the screen. After you are done you can check the leader board to see where you are ranked. The game will automatically sort the players numerically based on score after 5 people have played.
